The wrong metric

Most apps give every player the same score.

A striker and a holding midfielder are not the same player. A goalkeeper is not a winger. When every position chases the same number, the number stops meaning anything.

What is Pace

Pace isn't a score. It's velocity.

Pace measures the rate of your improvement — week over week, session over session — weighted to the position you play. Two players can have the same Training Score but very different Paces. The score tells you where you are. Pace tells you where you're going.

The 4-week block

One block. One link. Revocable.

A coach assembles four weeks of training — bookended by LSPT and LSST retests scored against FA England and US Soccer norms — and generates a private link. The parent opens it on any device: no app, no account, no login. The coach revokes it with one click when the block ends.
