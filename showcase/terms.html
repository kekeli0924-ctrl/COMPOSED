This is a pre-launch showcase.

This site describes software that is in development. Nothing on these pages constitutes an offer to sell, a subscription, or a binding service. Information shown may change before the pilot begins.

What you can do here

  • Read about how the Pace model is designed and weighted per position.
  • Request an invitation to the first pilot cohort — a small group of competitive youth teams.
  • Share the page with coaches and parents who might want to pilot.

What is not on offer yet

  • No user accounts, subscriptions, or purchases are available at this time.
  • No data has been collected from players. No training videos are being processed.
  • Mockups of Squad Pulse, the Pace dashboard, and the analysis board are illustrative and do not reflect real player data.

Accuracy

We try to describe the product honestly. Specific numbers shown in illustrations (roster sessions, percentages, frame counts) are representative, not measurements of real users. The dependency list in "Built With" reflects the actual stack as of the current build.

Pilot terms

If your team is accepted into the pilot, separate written terms will cover data handling, retention, and withdrawal. You can leave the pilot at any time; on withdrawal, the team's data will be deleted within 30 days.

Liability

Composed is provided as-is during the pilot. The training guidance in the app is informational and is not a substitute for qualified coaching, medical advice, or sports-science judgment. Club coaches remain the authority on player development.
